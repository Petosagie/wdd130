Home page

Ghomes Property is an indigenous firm. we’re building much more than just homes. We’re building trust. We believe trust is the foundation of every successful relationship. Building mutual trust with our partners is at the core of everything we do. We walk every day in their shoes, on their roads, to ensure they constantly feel how they deserve to feel. Comfortable. We are a front-line real estate development and management company located in Benin City metropolis, the heartbeat of Nigeria with great experience in delivering premium quality homes to hundreds of clients in search of style, comfort and value. Our company was founded in 2021 by Mr. Osagie Ohenhen with the aim to construct, sell or rent premium homes so as to curb the housing demand in Benin City and its environments. As one of the top Real Estate agents in the city, we have created a competitive edge for investors, buyers, and sellers by prioritizing their needs.
